Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Welcome to the Stardust Ecosystem (including Stardust Store and Stardust X Messenger). Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy details how we handle, store, and protect user data via our modern cloud infrastructure.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our authenticated applications, we collect standard credentials including Email addresses, usernames, and passwords provided during registration. User-generated content such as real-time messages are securely processed.

2. Cloud Infrastructure & Security

We utilize Google Firebase as our primary cloud database. Credentials are stored securely using SSL/TLS encryption. Passwords are automatically hashed via Firebase Authentication, ensuring no raw passwords can be viewed by anyone.

3. Retention & Deletion

Data is retained only as long as your account remains active. Users retain the full right to request deletion of their account records by contacting support.
